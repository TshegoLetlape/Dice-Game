GAME RULES

PLAYERS

  • This game consists of two player characters [SLYTHERIN AND GRYFFINDOR]
  • Each player will choose their own character between the two.

HOW TO PLAY?

  • The game has 6 plays, 3 for each player. The players should take turns after every play.
  • To throw the die, the player needs to click the [WINGARDIUM LEVIOSA] button.
  • The die is thrown randomly
  • The number thrown will be displayed in the box under the player's character name.
  • After a player's turn is over they click the [HOLD] button to allow another player their turn.
  • This game consists of Three rounds.
  • Each player gets to play atleast three rounds before the game is over.
  • The score of each round will be displayed in the [TOTAL] box.
  • After the last round, the player with the highest total wins, WINS the game.
  • The WINNER is displayed on the alert that appears at the top of the screen.
